Producer Collective - Features & Training Guide

This document lists all available "tags" (Producers, Instruments, Modifiers, etc.) and their functions. You can easily copy and paste this content into Google Docs or another word processor.

1. Producers

Producers offer a distinct sonic palette based on their iconic style. Each producer has a master volume and individual knobs for their signature "ingredients". Many also feature a unique "Spice" button for extra flavor.

Timbaland

  • Ingredients:
    • Bouncy, syncopated rhythm
    • Exotic world samples
    • Vocal chops and stutters
  • Spice (Beatbox Rhythm): with a human beatbox foundation

Dr. Dre

  • Ingredients:
    • G-Funk synth lead
    • Hard-hitting clean drums
    • Deep piano melody
  • Spice (Electric Guitar): with Dr. Dre style electric guitar licks and riffs

The Neptunes

  • Ingredients:
    • Minimalist quirky percussion
    • Spacey synth chords
    • Driving bassline
  • Spice (Electric Guitar): with Neptunes style electric guitar licks and riffs
  • Secondary Spice (No Chad): in the solo style of Pharrell Williams, focusing on his signature drum programming and melodic hooks

Scott Storch

  • Ingredients:
    • Lavish piano riff
    • Middle Eastern strings
    • Heavy 808s
  • Spice (Exotic Scale): using an exotic, middle-eastern scale

Kanye West

  • Ingredients:
    • Soulful vocal sample chop
    • Orchestral arrangement
    • Distorted synth bass
  • Spice (Chipmunk Soul): with a pitched-up soul sample

808 Mafia

  • Ingredients:
    • Dark trap bell melody
    • Rapid-fire hi-hats
    • Booming sub-bass
  • Spice (Soft Clipper): with an aggressive soft clipper on the master for loudness

Quincy Jones

  • Ingredients:
    • Lush jazz chords
    • Funky horn section
    • Live string ensemble
  • Spice (Room for God): with space for unexpected magic

Hans Zimmer

  • Ingredients:
    • Epic, sweeping orchestral swells
    • Driving, powerful percussion
    • Atmospheric, evolving synth textures
  • Spice (Low-end Brass): with a powerful low-end brass 'bwaaam' sound

Metro Boomin

  • Ingredients:
    • Ominous, dark synth melody
    • Hard-hitting 808 bass

  • Spice (Dark Atmosphere): with a dark, cinematic atmosphere

J Dilla

  • Ingredients:
    • Off-kilter, unquantized drums

    • Thick, walking bassline
  • Spice (Dilla Swing): with a lazy, behind-the-beat Dilla swing

Teddy Riley

    • Classic new jack swing rhythm
    • Punchy brass stabs and hits
    • Upbeat, funky synth bass
  • Spice (R&B/Hip-Hop Fusion): fusing R&B chords with hip-hop drums

Darkchild

  • Ingredients:
    • Futuristic, staccato synth stabs
    • Complex, syncopated drum programming
    • Layered vocal harmonies and ad-libs
  • Spice (Stutter Edit): with complex vocal stutter edits

DJ Quik

  • Ingredients:

    • Live-sounding, funky bassline
    • Classic talkbox vocal melody
  • Spice (G-Funk Whine): with a high-pitched G-Funk synthesizer whine

Questlove

    • Live in-the-pocket hip-hop drums
    • Earthy neo-soul groove
    • Layered, textured percussion
  • Spice (Live Feel): played with the feel of a live, in-the-pocket drummer

Zaytoven

  • Ingredients:
    • Signature gospel-influenced piano chords

Mustard

  • Spice (Ratchet Bounce): with a minimalist, ratchet music bounce

Jimi Hendrix

  • Ingredients:

    • Innovative use of feedback and wah effects
